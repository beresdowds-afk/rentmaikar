# Persona Integration

Persona is the platform's **identity verification** provider only.

Persona MUST NOT touch:

- eSIMs / SIM cards
- IoT devices, GPS trackers, telematics, dash cams, fuel/temperature sensors
- Vehicle connectivity or Hologram services

Persona provides:

- Customer / driver / owner KYC
- Government ID, passport, driver's licence verification
- Selfie and liveness checks
- Business verification (KYB)
- Compliance status and audit logs

See `docs/architecture/PersonaResponsibilities.md`,
`docs/architecture/IdentityVerificationArchitecture.md`,
and `docs/architecture/architectureRule.md`.
